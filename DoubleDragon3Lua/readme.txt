For use with "Double Dragon III - The Sacred Stones (U) [!].nes" rom with FCEUX
Written by sleepy - Shawn M. Crawford
6 January 2014
Displays Player Coordinates, HP, and Enemy HP stats on screen.

DoubleDragon3StatsDynamic.lua:
The HP stats follow the characters.

DoubleDragon3StatsStatic.lua
The HP stats are statically set in the upper left corner of the screen.

Noted offsets:

045D: 1P HP
045E: 2P HP

045F: E1 HP
0460: E2 HP

00B7: P1 X
00CB: P1 Y

00B8: P1 X
00CC: P1 Y

00B9: E1 X
00CD: E1 Y

00BA: E2 X
00CE: E2 Y
