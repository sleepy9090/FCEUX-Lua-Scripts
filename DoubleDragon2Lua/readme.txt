For use with "Double Dragon II - The Revenge (U) (PRG1) [!].nes" rom with FCEUX
Written by sleepy - Shawn M. Crawford
6 January 2014
Displays Player Coordinates, HP, and Enemy HP stats on screen.

DoubleDragonIIStatsDynamic.lua:
The HP stats follow the characters (lower right).

DoubleDragonIIStatsDynamicII.lua:
The HP stats follow the characters (top).

DoubleDragonIIStatsStatic.lua
The HP stats are statically set in the upper left corner of the screen.

Noted offsets:
041E: Player 1 HP
041F: Player 2 HP
0420: Enemy 1 HP
0421: Enemy 2 HP

0077: Player 1 X Coordinate
0092: Player 1 Y Coordinate

0078: Player 2 X Coordinate
0093: Player 2 Y Coordinate

0079: Enemy 1 X Coordinate
0094: Enemy 1 Y Coordinate

007A: Enemy 2 X Coordinate
0095: Enemy 2 Y Coordinate
