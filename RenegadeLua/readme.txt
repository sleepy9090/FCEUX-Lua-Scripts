For use with "Renegade (U) [!].nes" rom with FCEUX
Written by sleepy - Shawn M. Crawford
6 January 2014
Displays Player Coordinates, HP, and Enemy HP stats on screen.

RenegadeStatsDynamic.lua:
The HP stats follow the characters.

RenegadeStatsStatic.lua
The HP stats are statically set in the upper left corner of the screen.

Noted offsets:

007D: 1P/2P HP

007E: E1 HP
007F: E2 HP
0080: E3 HP

0046: P1 X
0055: P1 Y

0047: E1 X
0056: E1 Y

0048: E2 X
0057: E2 Y

0049: E3 X
0058: E3 Y
